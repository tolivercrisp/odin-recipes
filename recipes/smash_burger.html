Smash Burger Recipe

There's no better way to kick off the fourth of July than Toliver's famous Smash Burger. Crack a beer, grab your spatula, and get ready to mince a lot of pickles.

Ingredients

  • Brioche Buns
  • Ground Beef (1 pound)
  • Ground Bison (1 pound)
  • White American Cheese (sliced)
  • Yellow Onion
  • Bread & Butter Pickles
  • Lime Juice
  • Ketchup
  • Mayonaise
  • Yellow Mustard
