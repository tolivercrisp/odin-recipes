Vodka Pasta

Close the blinds and light that half-burnt Yankee candle you found underneath the bathroom sink: it's time for Toliver's famous Vodka Pasta recipe. Keep a shot glass handy (or don't), but remember, you'll still need some left for the pasta.

  • Pepper
